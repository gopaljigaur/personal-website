The Large Language Model (LLM) + Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot is a powerful tool that combines the capabilities of a large language model with the ability to retrieve and generate information from a knowledge base. This chatbot can provide detailed answers to a wide range of questions by leveraging the knowledge stored in a pre-trained model and additional information extracted from uploaded documents using RAG.
